If you’ve been feeling the weight of surveillance, corporate greed, and the slow creep of fascism online, this issue is your first step toward understanding the battle lines.

Learn how to run an offline copy of Wikipedia using Kiwix, dig into the end of net neutrality, and explore the tools you’ll need to keep control of your data in a world increasingly determined to take it from you. This issue isn’t just information; it’s a blueprint for resistance. If you’re ready to take action, keep reading. We’ve only just begun.
